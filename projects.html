ITINERARY ATLAS

Vacation Trip Planner

My Role: UI/UX Designer

Project Duration: 3 months

Target Audience

New Travellers

Experienced Travellers

Visitors to a new region

Problem Statement

It can be stressful to plan an itinerary for a trip, especially one to a new location.

Solution

Create a website where people can share their suggestions for traveling in a specific area. People can share their travel plans online, and other people can base their plans on those. Make traveling much less difficult and stressful overall.

Key Problems

One of the takeaways from our competitive analysis research, based on popular sources (internet forums such as Reddit and Rick Steve's website), is that there is a demand for itinerary sharing (particularly in the form of receiving tailored feedback on a tentative plan a particular user has); however, such feedback seems to be either lacking (as there are not enough reddit users in a given itinerary feedback community, ex. A place to discuss travel itineraries), or the community they post the itinerary does not specifically focus on itinerary advice (just travel in general, ex. Travel Hacks).

INTERACTIVE DESIGN

Explore Tab

User's are able to explore trending places or type the location into the "Destination" placeholder box.

Destination Page

This page would appear if users entered their destination in the explore page. Other users' itineraries are displayed and ranked by star ratings. Additionally, the itinerary includes an approximate cost for each activity.

Itinerary Tab

Users have the option of saving other users' itineraries. They can also look through popular itineraries created by other users.
